Diya Minerals –
The Art Of Marble & Granite

With a legacy spanning 25+ years, Diya Minerals is a trusted name in the Indian natural stone industry, and the principal company behind Diya Marbles and Diya Granites. From a modest start with a rented godown in Makrana, we’ve grown into a reliable force in Kishangarh – operating a granite manufacturing unit and a marble trading division that delivers excellence across India and abroad.

Our journey is rooted in experience, vision, and an uncompromising approach to quality. We specialize in sourcing the finest blocks directly from renowned quarries and processing them with precision to bring out their true strength and beauty. Every slab, tile, or cut-to-size piece that leaves our premises is a blend of nature’s elegance and our craftsmanship.

OUR DIRECTOR

The Pillars Behind Diya Minerals

MR. GOUTAM KABRA

- The Visionary Founder

“Success doesn’t start big — it starts with courage.”

It was in the dusty lanes of Makrana that Mr. Goutam Kabra laid the first brick of what is now Diya Minerals. With just a rented godown and an eye for quality, he began sourcing the purest Makrana marble. His relentless work ethic, integrity, and long-term relationships built the trust that still defines our brand today.

Today, his legacy stands strong — not just in stone, but in every business value we uphold.

MR. GOURAV KABRA

- The Strategic Expansionist

“A legacy grows only when it adapts to new opportunities.”

In 2008, Mr. Gourav Kabra, Goutam Ji’s younger brother, joined the business with a sharp sense of market expansion. He introduced granite to the company’s portfolio, recognizing the growing demand for this versatile stone. Under his leadership, Diya Minerals expanded into granite manufacturing, built advanced infrastructure in Kishangarh, and scaled operations to meet global standards.

Thanks to him, Diya Minerals became more than a trading company — it became a full-service stone brand.

MR. VEDANT KABRA

- The Next-Gen Torchbearer

“We are not just continuing a legacy — we are evolving it.”

Now stepping into this proud legacy, Mr. Vedant Kabra represents Gen Z with a global mindset. Young, ambitious, and deeply grounded in family values, Vedant brings a fresh design-focused perspective to the business — along with digital innovation, modern branding, and a clear focus on international growth.
